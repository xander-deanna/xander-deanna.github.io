About Me

Hi there! My name is Deanna. I'm a recent graduate from the Univerity of Texas - Austin Full-Stack Development Boot Camp. I'm originally from Pittsburgh, PA but moved to Austin in March of 2016. I love living in Texas, and I'm so glad to be a part of such a great city - especially a city so deeply immersed in tech. I created this webpage to help showcase the skills I have learned throughout my boot camp journey, but I've always had a love for web development. I've been using HTML and CSS since Myspace first dominated the internet, and still continue Front-End Development as a hobby long after it faded away. I also have a background in design, and am well versed in design tools like Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. Art and design have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I'm so excited to finally be able to expand upon my skills and pursue what I've always loved as a career. Please feel free to click "Contact" if you have any questions, and enjoy the site!
